I am a masters student at the University of Colorado Boulder. I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Bradley Hayes and be a part of the Collaborative Artificial Intelligence and Robotics Labotatory ([CAIRO Lab](https://www.cairo-lab.com/)). My research is at the intersection of Human-Robot-Interaction and Inverse Reinforcement Learning. I am particularly intested in learning how human preferences can be accounted for and used to improve efficiency in a shared task.

I completed my undergrad from Delhi Technological University in 2019, where I majored in 'Information Technology'. Prior to masters, I have worked as a Software Developer with Citicorp Services India Pvt. Ltd.

In my free time, I like to go for bike rides and runs along the Boulder Creek. I also enjoy playing the guitar and exploring new music.
